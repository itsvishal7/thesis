GDP_Uttar_Pradesh_District_level (2004-2005 to 2010-2011).csv
This csv contains district wise GDP (in RS. Cr.) and Growth Rate % year over year of Uttar Pradesh from 2004-05 to 2010-11.

Gross_State_Domestic_Product_State_level (2004-2005 to 2014)-2015.xls
